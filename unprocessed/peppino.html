Peppino

A member in Luigi Vampa's group of bandits. The Count of Monte Cristo saved Peppino from execution by persuading the Pope to pardon Peppino with a large emerald. He brought Albert de Morcerf's ransom letter to Baron Franz d'Épinay and the Count of Monte Cristo.
