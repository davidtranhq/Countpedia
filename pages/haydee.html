Haydée

    Daughter of the Greek Ali Pasha. She and her mother were betrayed and sold into slavery by Count de Morcerf. She would later be bought by the Count of Monte Cristo. When the Count exposes the Count de Morcerf's treachery, Haydée testifies and successfully convicts him.

    Later, the Count falls in love with her. They sail off into the Mediterranean.
