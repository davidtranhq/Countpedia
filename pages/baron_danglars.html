Baron Danglars

Family: Madame Danglars (wife), Eugénie Danglars (son)

Occupation: Banker

In the beginning, he worked as the purser of the Pharaon. His jealousy for Edmond Dantès pushes him to accuse Edmond of treason. After Edmond's imprisonment, Danglars fled Marseilles and acccumulated his wealth as a banker. He married Madame de Nargonne and settled in Paris.

A stock market crash induced by the Count of Monte Cristo destroys a large part of Danglar's fortune. Unwilling to repay a deposit he held for the hospitals, Danglars flees to Italy, where he is kidnapped by bandit leader Luigi Vampa. Vampa, friend of the Count, extorts Danglars of all but 50 000 francs. The Count anonymously returns the money to the hospitals and releases Danglars.
